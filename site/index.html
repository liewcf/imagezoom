Image Zoom

A small Chrome extension for zooming images on normal web pages. It has no popup, no account, no analytics, no storage, and no server.

View source Privacy policy

What it does

  • Double-click an unlinked image to open a zoom overlay.
  • Linked images keep their normal link click.
  • Scroll in the overlay to zoom and drag to pan.
  • Press Escape, double-click, or click the dark background to close.

What it avoids

  • No popup or settings page in version 0.1.3.
  • No analytics, accounts, cookies, or remote service.
  • No extra Chrome API permissions.

Status

Version 0.1.3 is prepared for Chrome Web Store submission. The public source and privacy policy are available now.
